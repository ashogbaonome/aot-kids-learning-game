Game ideas

Alphabet Match

Match letters to pictures (A → Apple). Builds letter recognition.

Counting Bubbles

Pop the right number of bubbles to match a target. Practice counting.

Shape Sorter

Drag or place shapes into matching slots. Teaches shapes & colors.

Memory Cards

Flip and match pairs. Builds memory and concentration.
